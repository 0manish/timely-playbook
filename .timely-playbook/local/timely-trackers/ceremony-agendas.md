# Ceremony agendas

## Weekly sync
- Review `.timely-playbook/local/.orchestrator/state.json` and
  `.timely-playbook/local/.orchestrator/STATUS.md`.
- Confirm open docs drift from `Timely-Governance-Trackers.md` and `AGENTS.md`.
- Resolve one backlog item for the next 72 hours.

## Gate review
- Confirm owner approvals for high-risk changes.
- Confirm fullstack or backlog evidence is present for impacted artifacts.
- Record governance decisions in
  `.timely-playbook/local/timely-trackers/agent-control-ledger.md`.

## Retro
- List what worked, what regressed, and what to automate next.
- Add follow-up backlog entries with owners and due dates.
- Re-export the template bundle if governance docs or guardrails changed.
